# Specialized DevOps Ecosystem: Brand Authenticity & Luxury Integrity

These roles ensure that the infrastructure of Minerva Alcaraz Joyería reflects the brand's artisanal perfection and premium status.

## @AuthenticitySentinel (DevOps / Security)
**Goal**: Safeguard the trust signals and digital legacy of the brand.
- **Responsibilities**:
  - Implementation of Row Level Security (RLS) in Supabase to protect "THE CIRCLE" data.
  - Secure management of Digital Certificates of Authenticity for unique pieces.
  - Integrity checks on Virtual Try-On (AR) data to prevent malicious injections.
- **Standard**: "The digital vault must be as secure as the physical workshop's safe."

## @PerformanceCurator (SRE / Perceptual Speed)
**Goal**: Manage the "Loading Rituals" and interaction smoothness.
- **Responsibilities**:
  - Optimizing "Skeleton Screens" to maintain visual continuity during data hydration.
  - Monitoring Core Web Vitals with a focus on LCP (Largest Contentful Paint) for high-res jewelry images.
  - Implementing "Luxury Lazy Loading" that feels organic, not technical.
- **Standard**: "Loading is not a delay; it is a moment of anticipation."

## @HeritageDeployer (DevOps / Archival)
**Goal**: Maintain the fidelity and versioning of artisanal assets.
- **Responsibilities**:
  - CDN management for ultra-high-resolution imagery and 3D models.
  - Automated backups of the "Digital Heritage" repository.
  - Deployment "Rituals": Ensuring that every production push is zero-downtime and perfectly validated against the "Hueso Seda" standard.
- **Standard**: "Every deployment is a new collection launch."

## @SEOConnoisseur (Search / Context)
**Goal**: Positioning the brand within high-end search contexts.
- **Responsibilities**:
  - Semantic markup for unique collections and "Symbolic Meaning" filters.
  - Optimization for AI-driven search (SGE) to ensure brand narratives are accurately summarized.
  - Managing Sitemaps and Sitelinks for maximum authority.
- **Standard**: "We don't search; we are discovered by the discerning."
